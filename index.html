URBAN SKIING

Learn to Cross-Country Ski and have fun exploring the capital of Scotland!

Most of the year there is no snow in Scotland. But this doesn't need to stop you Nordic skiing. Roller Skis are a great way to prepare for next winter and with many great bike paths in central Scotland they are fun in their own right.

Most of the equipment is identical with the exception of the skis themselves, which are replaced by the shorter more robust "Roller Skis" with wheels to allow use on roads and cycle paths.

COACH PETER

My name is Peter and I've been Cross-Country Skiing and Rollerskiing for over 15 years. I'm a Snowsport Scotland qualified Level 2 coach with experience coaching skiers at both the Fife Rollerski Club and the London XC Ski Club. You can see my full profile here at rollerski.co.uk.
