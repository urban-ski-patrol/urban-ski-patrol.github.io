About

Learn to Cross-Country Ski and have fun exploring the capital of Scotland!

Roller Skiing is the summer version of Nordic / Cross-Country Skiing. Most of the equipment is identical with the exception of the Skis, which are replaced by shorter more robust "Roller Skis" with wheels to let us move on roads and cycle paths.

My name is Peter and I've been Cross-Country Skiing and Rollerskiing for over 15 years. I'm a Snowsport Scotland qualified Level 2 coach with experience coaching skiers at both the Fife Rollerski Club and the London XC Ski Club. You can see my full profile here at rollerski.co.uk.

Fill out the form or e-mail peter@urban-ski-patrol.co.uk to find out more and book a session!
